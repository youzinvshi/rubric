## Global Claim Discipline

- Use minimal C1/C2 only for Section 2 motivation and robustness; do not treat them as real-run method-result gates.
- Do not write SFT+GRPO coverage, dimension-level recovery, downstream utility, ablation, or semantic-space claims until the corresponding real C0-C14 evidence rows are safe_to_claim.
- A BSC coverage change alone is metric evidence, not dimension-level recovery or judge-utility evidence.

## Claim Ladder Milestones

| Level | Required Claims | Evidence Required | Downgrade Rule |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| motivation | C1, C6 | Frozen 100-example hard-gold diagnostic with C1 and threshold-robustness C6 gates. | Does not support any trained-method claim by itself. |
| metric-support | C0, C2, C3 | Hard-gold BSC coverage change with stable redundancy and hallucination under the fixed protocol. | Without downstream support, report metric-only BSC evidence. |
| method-support | C5, C7, C14 | SFT-only vs SFT+GRPO and reward-component ablations pass C5/C7/C14. | Without C14, report proxy-gold supervision evidence rather than RLVR evidence. |
| judge-utility support | C0, C4, C9, C10, C12 | RewardBench, RewardBench-2, and JudgeBench API/model scorer rows pass C4/C9/C10. | Without C12, write aggregate coverage change rather than dimension-level recovery; without C0, no trained-method row is paper-facing. |
